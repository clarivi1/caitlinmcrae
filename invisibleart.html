Invisible Art

Project Description

Invisible art was inspired by a quote by Zuzana Licko. My concept was Typography is an art form. Looking at type as art. I decided to focus on this topic after I found a quote Zuzana had said stating ‘It’s not a problem of being a woman in a man’s world. It’s being a type designer in a world that gives little recognition to this art form’. This made me feel inspired. I felt that I needed to grab the attention of the viewer and help them realize the beauty and hidden/unrecognized art in typography, which is why I chose the tag line: “Invisible Art”. This was for a fictional lecture series.
