Checkmake

Project Description

This was an app I came up with for a UX project. I used the waterfall method. I completed every stage before continuing to the next. I tested this app on my target audience and often referred to my personas. “For those that often think that they have all the ingredients, but later find that they are missing one. For those who are easily distracted or often multi-tasking. Most of all, for those that don’t have much time in the day, but still want a nice quick meal. Checkmake is the app for you.”
