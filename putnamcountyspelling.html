Theater Poster

Project Description

I designed this theater poster for “The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee” Play. After meeting with the client, I decided to create the hierarchy as a large silhouette image of one of the contestants. From a far the viewer will just see a textured character and up close they will see the words flowing through the character. This was a two-color overprint job, as you can see in the postcard. That image shows the piece prior to overprinting.
